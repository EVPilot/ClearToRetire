About ClearToRetire

ClearToRetire helps federal employees plan their retirement with confidence. Calculate your FERS pension, TSP withdrawals, Social Security benefits, and net take-home income — all in one place.

Built specifically for FERS employees, including special provision categories: Air Traffic Controllers (ATC), Law Enforcement Officers (LEO), Firefighters (FF), and Customs & Border Protection Officers (CBPO).

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who is ClearToRetire for?

A: ClearToRetire is designed for all FERS (Federal Employees Retirement System) employees, including regular FERS and special provision categories (ATC, LEO, FF, CBPO). It is not designed for CSRS employees.

Q: Does ClearToRetire collect my data?

A: No. ClearToRetire processes everything locally on your device. No personal information, financial data, or retirement details are ever transmitted to any server. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Q: How accurate are the calculations?

A: ClearToRetire uses the same formulas and constants published by OPM, SSA, and IRS. However, actual benefits are subject to official determination by federal agencies. Use this app for planning purposes and verify critical numbers with your HR office.

Q: What tax calculations are included?

A: The app calculates federal income tax using current brackets and includes state-specific rules. Many states exempt federal pensions and/or Social Security from state income tax. The app accounts for these exemptions based on your state of residence.

Q: Can I compare different retirement dates?

A: Yes. The Compare tab lets you view two retirement years side-by-side, showing differences in pension, TSP balance, Social Security, net income, and more. The Dashboard also has a what-if slider to instantly adjust your retirement year.

Q: What is the FERS Supplement?

A: The Special Retirement Supplement (SRS) is a bridge payment for eligible FERS employees who retire before age 62. It approximates Social Security benefits based on your federal service and stops at age 62. ClearToRetire calculates this automatically based on your eligibility.

Q: What iOS version do I need?

A: ClearToRetire requires iOS 17.0 or later.
